All India Bakchod is a Mumbai-based comedy group, and was the name of their podcast, YouTube channel and production company. The name is a parody of All India Radio, with bakchod being Hindi slang for 'dumb fuck'. It began in 2012 as a humorous/pop-culture podcast by comedians Tanmay Bhat and Gursimran Khamba.

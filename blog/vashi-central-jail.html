By Abhishek Chaudhary

Published 2020-05-23

Fr Conceicao Rodrigues institute of technology A.K.A. Vashi Central Jail is an engineering college in Navi Mumbai, It is famous for treating it's prisoners like students, They serve 4 years in the prison wherein they are made to do various menial tasks, such as completing assignments and giving re-tests, re-prelims etc.
